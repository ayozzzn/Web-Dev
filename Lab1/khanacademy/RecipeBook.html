Recipe #1: Classic Pasta Carbonara

  • Preparation Time: 15 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 15 minutes
  • Serves: 4 people

Ingredients:

Ingredient Quantity
Spaghetti 400g
Eggs 4 large
Pancetta or guanciale 150g
Pecorino Romano cheese 100g (grated)
Black pepper Freshly ground, to taste
Salt For pasta water

Instructions:

  1. Step 1: Bring a large pot of salted water to boil for the pasta.
  2. Step 2: Cut pancetta into small cubes and cook in a pan until crispy.
  3. Step 3: In a bowl, whisk eggs with grated pecorino and plenty of black pepper.
  4. Step 4: Cook spaghetti according to package instructions until al dente.
  5. Step 5: Reserve a cup of pasta water, then drain spaghetti.
  6. Step 6: Quickly mix hot pasta with pancetta (off heat), then stir in egg mixture.
  7. Step 7: Add pasta water as needed to create creamy sauce. Serve immediately!

Source: BBC Good Food - Carbonara Recipe

Recipe #2: Moist Chocolate Cake

  • Preparation Time: 20 minutes
  • Baking Time: 35 minutes
  • Serves: 8-10 people

Ingredients:

Ingredient Quantity
All-purpose flour 2 cups
Sugar 2 cups
Cocoa powder ¾ cup
Baking powder 2 tsp
Baking soda 1.5 tsp
Eggs 2 large
Milk 1 cup
Vegetable oil ½ cup
Boiling water 1 cup

Instructions:

  1. Step 1: Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch pans.
  2. Step 2: Mix dry ingredients (flour, sugar, cocoa, baking powder, baking soda).
  3. Step 3: Add eggs, milk, and oil. Beat on medium speed for 2 minutes.
  4. Step 4: Stir in boiling water (batter will be thin - that's normal!).
  5. Step 5: Pour into prepared pans and bake for 30-35 minutes.
  6. Step 6: Cool in pans for 10 minutes, then transfer to wire racks.
  7. Step 7: Frost with your favorite chocolate frosting when completely cool.

Source: Allrecipes - Chocolate Cake

Recipe #3: Authentic Greek Salad

  • Preparation Time: 20 minutes
  • Chilling Time: 30 minutes (optional)
  • Serves: 4 people

Ingredients:

Ingredient Quantity
Tomatoes (ripe) 4 large
Cucumber 1 large
Red onion 1 medium
Green bell pepper 1
Kalamata olives ½ cup
Feta cheese 200g
Olive oil ¼ cup
Dried oregano 1 tbsp
Salt & pepper To taste

Instructions:

  1. Step 1: Wash and chop tomatoes into wedges.
  2. Step 2: Peel cucumber if desired and cut into thick slices.
  3. Step 3: Thinly slice red onion and green bell pepper.
  4. Step 4: Combine all vegetables in a large salad bowl.
  5. Step 5: Add olives and crumble feta cheese on top.
  6. Step 6: Drizzle with olive oil, sprinkle oregano, salt, and pepper.
  7. Step 7: Toss gently just before serving. Can be chilled for 30 minutes.

Source: The Mediterranean Dish - Greek Salad
